McDonalds for the dietary restricted

Many common chain restaurants are accommodating for the basic american diet. Rarely do these restaurants have gluten-free, nut-free, vegan, or dairy-free options. Although, the majority of US households have someone with dietary restrictions. It can be tricky to eat out when most common restaurants do not accommodate for dietary restrictions. Listed below are items from the Mcdonalds menu that those with dietary restrictions can eat.

Vegan:

  • Fruit and maple oatmeal - with no cream
  • Apple slices
  • Southwest salad- with no chicken or cheese
  • Side Salad- with balsamic vinaigrette
  • McCafe Coffee
  • Minute Maid slushie
  • Apple pie
